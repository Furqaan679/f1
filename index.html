**The Little Cloud** Once upon a time, a small, fluffy cloud named Piko floated high in the sky. Piko loved to drift over fields, mountains, and oceans, watching the world below. One day, Piko saw a group of flowers wilting in a dry meadow. They looked sad and thirsty. “I want to help,” Piko said, and he squeezed himself tightly until—plop!—a tiny raindrop fell.


Then another. And another. Soon, the whole meadow was dancing in the rain. The flowers lifted their heads and smiled at the sky. Piko felt happy, knowing he’d made a difference. From that day on, he drifted with a purpose, looking for places that needed a little rain and a little kindness. And the world below bloomed wherever he went.
